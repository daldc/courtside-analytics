# Courtside Analytics
### Moneyball for College Basketball Recruiting

---

## The Problem

Every spring, **2,300+ basketball players** enter the NCAA transfer portal. Coaches have roughly **two weeks** to evaluate hundreds of candidates, identify fits, and make offers — all while competing against programs with 10x their budget.

**Mid-major programs are outgunned.** They can't afford Synergy's enterprise platform. They don't have dedicated analytics staff. And they're scouting the same 50 "known" portal names as everyone else while **hundreds of high-value players go unnoticed**.

Meanwhile, **international talent is flooding into college basketball** — Illinois built an Elite Eight team on European imports — but most programs have zero infrastructure for overseas scouting.

---

## The Solution

**Courtside Analytics** is an AI-powered recruiting intelligence platform that helps mid-major programs **find undervalued talent** across the transfer portal, JUCO, and international leagues.

Think of it as **Moneyball for college basketball recruiting.**

We analyze thousands of player stat profiles across every level — D1, D2, JUCO, NAIA, and international leagues — and surface the players that traditional scouting misses.

---

## How It Works

**🔍 Hidden Gem Engine**
Our algorithm scores every portal entry on a 0-100 scale based on per-minute production, efficiency metrics, conference-adjusted performance, and improvement trajectory. A player averaging 18 PPG at a low-visibility D2 program with elite efficiency? He shows up at the top of your feed before 247Sports even notices him.

**🌍 International Scout**
Stats from 50+ international leagues (EuroLeague, ACB, BBL, NBL, FIBA Youth) translated into NCAA-comparable metrics. Find the next Kasparas Jakucionis without flying to Lithuania.

**🎯 Roster Fit Analysis**
Tell us what your program needs — a stretch 4 who shoots 37%+ from three, or a point guard with a 2.5+ AST/TO ratio — and we filter every available player to match your specific roster gaps.

**⚡ Real-Time Portal Alerts**
New portal entries instantly scored and delivered to your inbox. "5 new players match your program's needs" — every morning during the portal window.
